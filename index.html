Smart Energy Meter

A smart meter is the next generation of a gas and electricity meter. They're being installed by energy suppliers in every home in England, Scotland, and Wales. Smart meters show you exactly how much energy you're using in Rupees, in near real time and will bring an end to estimated bills.

FEATURES

Automated energy readings using IOT technologies from anywhere,any place and at any time
Pay your electricity bill from the convinience of your own home or on the go
